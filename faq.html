General Questions

Most Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book an appointment?

Yes. We recommend booking your appointment in advance, especially during weekends and wedding seasons.

Do you accept walk-in customers?

Yes, walk-ins are welcome depending on availability. Appointments receive priority.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash, UPI, Google Pay, PhonePe and major debit & credit cards.

Do you provide home bridal makeup services?

Yes. Bridal makeup services at your location can be arranged with prior booking.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes. Please inform us at least 24 hours before your appointment.

How early should I book my bridal makeup?

We recommend booking at least 1–3 months in advance, especially during the wedding season.

Do you provide HD and Airbrush Makeup?

Yes. We offer HD Makeup, Airbrush Makeup, Traditional Bridal Makeup and customized bridal packages.

Can I book a bridal makeup trial?

Absolutely. Trial sessions help finalize your preferred look before the wedding day.

Do you help with saree draping and jewellery setting?

Yes. Bridal packages include professional saree draping, hairstyling and jewellery setting.

How long does a Hair Spa take?

Usually between 45 and 60 minutes depending on your hair condition.

Is Keratin Treatment safe?

Yes. We use professional salon-grade products and recommend after-care instructions for the best results.

How long does Hair Smoothening last?

Typically 4–6 months depending on your hair type and maintenance routine.

Which hair color brands do you use?

We use premium salon-quality brands to ensure safe, vibrant and long-lasting color.

Is Hydra Facial suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes. Hydra Facial is suitable for most skin types, including sensitive skin.

How often should I get a facial?

Every 4–6 weeks is generally recommended to maintain healthy and glowing skin.

Will De-Tan Treatment remove permanent pigmentation?

De-Tan treatments reduce sun tan. For pigmentation, our specialists can recommend suitable skincare solutions.

How long does a Hydra Facial take?

A Hydra Facial usually takes between 45 and 60 minutes.

Can I pay online?

Yes. We accept UPI, Google Pay, PhonePe, bank transfers and major cards.

Do you offer gift vouchers?

Yes. Gift vouchers are available for birthdays, anniversaries and special occasions.

Do you provide membership discounts?

Yes. Members enjoy exclusive discounts, priority booking and seasonal offers.

Can I cancel my appointment?

Yes. Kindly notify us at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment.

Do you provide consultation before treatment?

Yes. Every service begins with a consultation to understand your preferences and requirements.

Do you offer bridal packages for destination weddings?

Yes. Destination bridal makeup services can be arranged. Please contact us to discuss your requirements.
